人口學詞典檢索系統
Demographic Dictionary Search System

項目介紹
Project Introduction

這是一個基於繁體中文的人口學詞典檢索系統，該系統是免費且開放的。Demopædia 是由聯合國人口司和國際人口學會支持的一個多語種、開源的人口學百科全書項目。
This is a traditional Chinese-based demographic dictionary search system that is free and open-source. Demopædia is a multilingual, open-source demography encyclopedia project supported by the United Nations Population Division and the International Union for the Scientific Study of Population.

本項目旨在基於 Demopædia 提供的 2024 年的第二版中文人口學詞典和 2013 年的第二版英文人口學詞典，製作一個簡體中文、繁體中文和英文的人口學詞典對照知識庫，為學生、教師、教授、研究人員、記者、政府相關部門和廣大公眾提供一個便捷的人口學專業術語查詢平台。
This project aims to create a simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, and English demographic dictionary knowledge base based on the 2024 Chinese and 2013 English demographic dictionaries provided by Demopædia. It provides a convenient platform for students, teachers, professors, researchers, journalists, relevant government departments, and the general public to search for professional demographic terminology.

儘管 Demopædia 提供了包括 14 種語言的人口學詞典查詢項目，這些功能可以滿足中文、英文等多語言專業術語的檢索需求，但在中文環境下仍然存在一些不便：
Although Demopædia provides demographic dictionary search projects in 14 languages, which can meet the retrieval needs of professional terms in multiple languages such as Chinese and English, there are still some inconveniences in the Chinese context:

  • 缺乏中英對照檢索內容
    Lack of Chinese-English cross-reference search content
  • 缺乏繁體中文的人口學詞典
    Lack of a traditional Chinese demographic dictionary

本項目可以解決這些問題，方便用戶快速檢索術語。
This project can solve these problems and facilitate users to quickly search for terms.
